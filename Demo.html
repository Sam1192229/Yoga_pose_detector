PoseNet Detector Demo Documentation

The PoseNet Detector Demo is a simple web application that utilizes the PoseNet model to detect human poses in real-time using a webcam. It overlays small placeholder images on the detected keypoints of the human body.

Usage

  1. Click on the Get Started button in the home tab.
  2. Ensure that your device has a working webcam connected.
  3. Upon opening the page, you should see a video stream from your webcam displayed on the screen. The PoseNet model will start detecting poses in real-time.

Interacting with the Demo

Once the demo is running, you can interact with it in the following ways:

  • Pose Detection: The PoseNet model detects various key points on the human body, such as the nose, eyes, shoulders, elbows, wrists, hips, knees, and ankles.
  • Placeholder Images: When a pose is detected, small placeholder images are overlaid on the video stream.
